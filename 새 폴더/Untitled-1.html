History

Manchester United Football Club is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. The club was formed in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR F.C. and changed its name to Manchester United in 1902. The club has won numerous trophies in English and European football, including a record 20 League titles, 12 FA Cups, and 3 UEFA Champions Leagues.
